## Games and Buy-Ins

### Creating a Game
- Game creators set the buy-in amount, daily goal, required days per week, duration, and apps to track
- By creating a game, you agree to pay the buy-in amount and participate in the game under the terms you set

### Joining a Game
- By joining a game, you agree to pay the buy-in amount and participate under the game's terms
- Buy-in payments are non-refundable once a game has started
- If a game is cancelled before it starts, buy-ins will be refunded

### Winning and Payouts
- Participants who meet their daily screen time goals for the required number of days per week for the full game duration are considered winners
- Winners split the total pool minus MyROI's platform fee (10%) equally
- If all participants meet their goals, each participant receives their buy-in back minus the platform fee
- Payouts are processed via Stripe within 5-7 business days of game completion

### Platform Fee
- MyROI charges a 10% platform fee on the total pool of each completed game
- This fee is deducted before winnings are distributed to winners
