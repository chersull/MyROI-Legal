# Privacy Policy for MyROI

**Last Updated: June 10, 2026**

## Introduction

MyROI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the MyROI mobile application ("the App").

By using MyROI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and password.
- **Game Information:** When you create or join a game, we collect the game parameters you set, including buy-in amount, daily goal, and duration.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Screen Time Data:** With your explicit permission, MyROI accesses Apple Screen Time data to verify your daily usage of apps selected for your accountability games. This data is used solely to track progress toward your game goals and is never sold or shared with third parties.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you use the App, including features accessed and actions taken.

### Payment Information
- **Payment Processing:** MyROI uses Stripe to process payments. We do not store your credit card or bank account information on our servers. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe. You can review Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Create and manage your MyROI account
- Facilitate game creation, participation, and completion
- Verify your daily progress toward your game goals using Screen Time data
- Process buy-in payments and distribute winnings
- Send you notifications related to your games (with your permission)
- Improve and maintain the App
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Firebase/Google:** We use Google Firebase for authentication, database storage, and cloud functions. Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
- **Stripe:** Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We share transaction-related information with Stripe to process payments. You can review Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.
- **Other Players:** Your display name and game progress may be visible to other participants in games you join.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

---

## Screen Time Data

MyROI requests access to Apple's Screen Time API to verify your daily app usage as part of your accountability game goals. Specifically:

- Screen Time access is requested only with your explicit consent
- We access only the app usage data for apps you have selected as part of your game
- Screen Time data is used solely to verify game progress
- We do not share Screen Time data with any third parties
- You may revoke Screen Time access at any time in your iPhone Settings

---

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address below.
